Serial Lookup & Model Identification

Enter your Diamond Back frame serial number to identify the manufacturer, production date and model details.

Important
  • Enter the serial exactly as stamped on the frame
  • No spaces are required (spaces will be automatically removed)
  • Frame Serials are typically located on the underside of the bottom bracket shell or inside rear dropout
Supported Serial Formats
  • Jere — Example: ABC123
  • Koizumi VM — Example: VM12345
  • Koizumi Kanji — Example: 1A1234
  • Fairly — Example: F2034567
